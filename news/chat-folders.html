Chat Folders

Status: Internal testing

A new commit to Signal for Android reveals that chat folders are coming to Signal! This new feature will allow you to organize your chats into different folders, each with their own name.


Here's a first look at chat folders thanks to screenshots from @cyrus

Please note that this feature is unreleased and in active development, so the final design and functionality may change.
